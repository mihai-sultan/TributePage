Free Code Camp

Open source comunity

Free Code Camp is an open source community that helps you learn how to code by working though self-paced coding challenges and building projects. It offers verified certificates, is completely free and you can help non-profits as you learn. It is also highly popular on GitHub.

You work through self-paced coding challenges, build projects, and earn certificates. You also will be connect with people in your city so you can code together.

Once you finish the first 1,200 hours of challenges, you’ll get to build a series of solutions for nonprofits. You’ll work in pairs, under the supervision of a volunteer project manager and a stakeholder from the nonprofit.

It takes about 2,080 hours to complete the Full Stack Developer certificate. This translates into one year of full-time coding. Once you've mastered all four certifications you should feel well prepared for tackling that elusive job interview. FreeCodeCamp's organization shines again, courtesy of the helping facilities that it makes available to its students, during their studying as well as afterwards.
